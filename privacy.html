1. Introduction

Welcome to Roblox Dev Network. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Discord server verification system.

2. Information We Collect

When you verify your account with our service, we collect:

  • Discord Information: Your Discord User ID and username
  • Roblox Information: Your Roblox User ID, username, and profile picture
  • Gameplay Data: Time spent playing games registered in our network (only for participating games)
Important: We do NOT collect your passwords, email addresses, or any sensitive personal information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Verify your identity and link your Discord account to your Roblox account
  • Track your participation in game testing for our points system
  • Display your username and basic profile information in our Discord server
  • Facilitate the game developer network and points exchange system

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • With your explicit consent
  • Anonymous gameplay statistics may be shared with game developers in our network

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data at any time
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out: Stop participating in the network by leaving the Discord server

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users aged 13 and above, in compliance with Discord's Terms of Service and Roblox's policies. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Third-Party Services

Our service uses:

  • Roblox OAuth2: For secure account verification
  • Discord API: For server integration
  • Supabase: For secure data storage

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.
